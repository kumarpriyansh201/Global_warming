Global Warming

Global warming is the gradual increase in the Earth's average surface temperature due to rising levels of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide, in the atmosphere. This increase in temperature has a number of negative impacts on the planet, including rising sea levels, more frequent and severe weather events, and damage to ecosystems and wildlife.


Causes of Global Warming

  • Carbon dioxide emissions from burning fossil fuels
  • Methane emissions from agriculture and waste
  • Deforestation and land use changes

Effects of Global Warming

  • Rising sea levels
  • More frequent and severe weather events
  • Damage to ecosystems and wildlife

Solutions to Global Warming

  • Transition to renewable energy sources
  • Reduce energy consumption and increase efficiency
  • Protect and restore ecosystems
