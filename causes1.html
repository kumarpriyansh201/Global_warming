CAUSES OF GLOBAL WARMING

Usage of vehicles

"Cars and trucks emit gases like Carbon dioxide and nitrogen oxides."

Deforestation

"When trees are cut, the stored carbon is released into the atmosphere as Carbon dioxide which leads to global warming."

Fossil fuel burning

"Burning of fossil fuels release carbon dioxide which is a greenhouse gas, into the air."

Development of industry

"Certain chemical reactions used in product manufacturing produce greenhouse gases."

Oil Drilling

"Oil drilling is responsible for 30% of the methane population and around 8% carbon dioxide pollution."

Oil and Gas

"The use of fossil fuels is also a threat to wildlife and the surrounding environments, because of the toxicity it kills off plant life."
